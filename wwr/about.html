Splash! White Water Rafting

Our mission is to provide a fun, memorable, and safe white water rafting experience.

History

Splash! White Water Rafting Company has been providing exceptional white water rafting experiences for over 25 years. Our founder, Ted Smith, built this company from his love of rafting and because he had a desire to share that experience with others. We are proud to have been able to host over 4,200 day and multiple-day trips for 25,000 guests over the years.
